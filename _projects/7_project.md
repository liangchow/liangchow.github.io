<b>quotebyte</b> is a full-stack responsive web app I launched in January 2026, marking my first 0 &rarr; 1 product. Users can subscribe to a weekly newsletter featuring quotes from scientists and engineers, as well as share their favorite quotes with others. The goal was to create a simple yet engaging experience that blends curated content with user contributions.

The tech stacks include `Node.js (Express)` for backend and `Firebase` for database. I also implemented CSRF protection and basic obfuscation techniques to add an extra layer of security.    

You can visit <b>quotebyte</b> [here](https://www.quotebyte.app/).
